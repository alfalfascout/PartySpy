A note about journals:

Journal parsing is entirely optional. Enabling journal parsing will enable more detailed statistics, but journals contain some personal information (computer username, graphics card info, CPUID, etc). I super promise not to mine your data for information (the information never leaves your machine), but if you're uncomfortable with giving the program access you don't have to use it. You will miss out on some neat features though.

Your journals are probably located at C:\Users\<Your Username>\AppData\Local\SpyParty\journals
